Communicate your research: Step I

Think about your research!

Now, try to simplify and reduce it to its most meaningful results or take home messages.

Put it in an accessible and easy to understand format!

It can be a chart, plot, equation, conceptual model, image.

You will be able o put a title, short description and representative keywords to your file.

Before submitting it we need to verify your credentials first. When you are aproved, you can upload your findings!

Communicate your research: Step II

Other users will start to look at your findings! Based on their interests users will receive notifications once a new research trailer is uploaded. This facilitates the exposure of your work.

Communicate your research: Step III

One or more users interested in your work will have the option to start a conversation tread. You can also invite other users to join. Anyone interested may apply to join an existent tread or form a new one. Here, you can exchange ideas and opinions about your findings. This way a very fruitful colaboration may begin faster than you expect.
