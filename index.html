Our aim: Development of the scientific community

The modern society became more aware of the importance of research. From skyscraper architecture to DNA alteration, from astrophysics to intermolecular interactions humanity is pushing the boundaries of science further and further. Thousands of research articles are being produced daily. Unfortunately, the process of filtering good and bad research takes a long time and can be very tedious. As a consequence publication process it is slowed down by months, years and sometimes valuable pieces of work never see the light of day. Through our online platform, Railer, we are trying to increase the efficiency of science communication by making research results available before publication.

Railer aims to be the first online platform of research results and ideas. Researchers will have the option to either upload or search through our database. The search can be done using simple keywords that define your interests. This is a fast and efficient way of filtering previously research uploads which you can sort by date, author, institution or even country. In order to upload, you can select a figure, an image or an interesting expression that you developed and load it on our website.

Are you worried about safety? Don’t be! When you upload your work, we are binding it to your personal account, therefore no one can claim it, but yourself. In addition, we are storing our data using blockchain technology therefore hacking the database to find and download your research is almost impossible.

    Frequently asked questions

    What areas of research can I find on Railer

    Our platform accepts uploads representative for any kind of research. Whether it is science, engineering or literature you will be able to find or share it here.

    How do I know I am looking at a good piece of research?

    Railer wants to enhance the speed at which we share ideas in the research world. When you discovered a new result you should attempt to analyse and perhaps discuss it with the author first before using it in your own work. Furthermore, all the users are asked to proove credentials first before uploading data.

    How do I know my data is secure?

    Railer uses the new and exciting blockchain technology which increases the security of our servers.

    Will I still be able to publish the data after I upload it on Railer?

    In the vast majority of cases, yes. Most research journals accept data if they were not released previously in their final form. We also offer you helpful links to other platforms that can tell you the publication conditions of your target journal.

    What if I want to delete one of my previous uploads?

    Users will have the posibility of selecting what uploads are visible to other users. This way you can shadow any unwanted pieces of work.

    Who has control/ownership of my work?

    Just you! Railer does not own any intelectual rights over the data being upoaded.

    Is Railer a replacement for a scientific journal publication?

    Absolutely not. Railer aims to increase the velocity of sharing ideas by distributing simple research trailers. A journal will still be the place to look for a comprehensive explanation of someone's work.

    How does blockchain technolgy make my data more secure?

    This new technology allows the data to be stored on multiple computers, not on a single driver as it conventionally was.
